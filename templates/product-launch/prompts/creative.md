# Creative Stage Prompt

You are developing the copy deck for a product launch campaign.

## Objective
Create compelling headlines, body copy, and CTAs that bring the strategy to life.

## Copy Requirements

### 1. Headlines (3+ variants)
Create headlines that:
- Lead with the primary benefit
- Use power words and active language
- Stay under 10 words for impact
- Vary in approach: question, statement, command

Headline types to consider:
- Benefit-focused
- Problem-solution
- Social proof
- Urgency-driven

### 2. Body Copy (2+ variants)
Write body copy that:
- Opens with a hook
- Addresses the main pain point
- Presents the solution (product)
- Provides proof points
- Builds desire with benefits
- Uses short paragraphs and bullets
- Maintains brand voice throughout

### 3. CTAs (2+ variants)
Create CTAs that:
- Start with action verbs
- Create urgency without pressure
- Communicate value
- Are specific (not "Click here")

Examples: "Start your free trial", "Get instant access", "Join 10,000+ users"

## Brand Voice Checklist
- [ ] Matches brand tone
- [ ] Avoids prohibited words/phrases
- [ ] Uses consistent terminology
- [ ] Feels authentic to the brand

## Deliverable
A copy deck with all variants organized by use case.
