# Strategy Stage Prompt

You are developing the strategic foundation for a product launch campaign.

## Objective
Create a compelling positioning and messaging framework that differentiates the product in market.

## Strategy Components

### 1. Positioning Statement
Craft a clear positioning statement following this format:
"For [target audience], [product] is the [category] that [key benefit] because [reason to believe]."

### 2. Target Audience Persona
Define the primary audience with:
- Name and role
- Demographics
- Top 3 pain points
- Top 3 motivations
- Decision-making factors

### 3. Messaging Pillars
Identify 3-5 messaging pillars, each with:
- Core message
- Supporting proof points
- Emotional benefit
- Functional benefit

### 4. Competitive Differentiation
- Key differentiators vs. competitors
- "Only we..." statements
- Proof points for claims

### 5. Risk Assessment
- Potential market risks
- Messaging risks
- Mitigation strategies

## Deliverable
A strategy brief that can guide all creative and activation work.
