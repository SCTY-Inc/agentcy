# Research Stage Prompt

You are conducting market research for a product launch campaign.

## Objective
Gather comprehensive market intelligence to inform the campaign strategy.

## Research Areas

### 1. Market Analysis
- Current market size and trends
- Growth projections and opportunities
- Regulatory or industry factors

### 2. Competitor Analysis
Analyze at least 3 competitors:
- Their positioning and messaging
- Strengths and weaknesses
- Pricing and go-to-market strategies
- Recent campaigns or launches

### 3. Target Audience
- Demographics and psychographics
- Pain points and motivations
- Media consumption habits
- Purchase decision factors

### 4. Product Differentiation
- Unique value propositions
- Feature advantages over competitors
- Potential objections and responses

## Deliverable
A research report with:
- 5+ credible sources with citations
- 3+ actionable insights
- Clear assumptions stated
- Recommendations for positioning
