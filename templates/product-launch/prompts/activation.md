# Activation Stage Prompt

You are developing the go-to-market activation plan for a product launch.

## Objective
Create a comprehensive channel strategy with calendar, budget, and KPIs.

## Activation Components

### 1. Channel Selection (2-4 channels)
Select channels based on:
- Where target audience spends time
- Budget efficiency
- Content format fit
- Measurement capability

Consider:
- Paid Social (LinkedIn, Meta, TikTok)
- Paid Search (Google, Bing)
- Content Marketing (Blog, SEO)
- Email Marketing
- Influencer/Creator partnerships
- PR and earned media
- Events (virtual/in-person)

### 2. Channel Strategy
For each channel, define:
- Objective (awareness, consideration, conversion)
- Target audience segment
- Key messages and content formats
- Budget allocation (% of total)
- Success metrics

### 3. Content Calendar
Create a 30-day launch calendar with:
- Specific dates
- Channel
- Content type
- Key message/theme
- Owner/status

Include:
- Pre-launch teasers
- Launch day activities
- Post-launch sustain content

### 4. Budget Allocation
- Total budget breakdown by channel
- Production costs vs. media spend
- Contingency allocation

### 5. KPIs and Targets
Define 3-5 KPIs with specific targets:
- Awareness: impressions, reach, video views
- Engagement: CTR, engagement rate, shares
- Conversion: leads, sign-ups, purchases
- Efficiency: CPM, CPC, CAC

## Deliverable
A complete activation plan ready for execution.
